Frequently Asked Questions

What is Verbeo AI?
Verbeo AI is an enterprise-grade voice AI platform that automates complex phone conversations with human-like latency, emotion, and intelligence. It replaces traditional call centers with autonomous agents that can handle millions of calls simultaneously.
Does it offer a free trial?
Yes! Our "Pay As You Go" plan includes 10 free minutes of call time so you can test the latency and voice quality with zero commitment.
Can we integrate with current tools?
Absolutely. Verbeo integrates seamlessly with Salesforce, HubSpot, Zapier, Slack, and any system with a REST API. We fit directly into your existing tech stack.
What languages does it speak?
Verbeo supports over 30 languages with native-level fluency and accent awareness, allowing you to deploy global support agents instantly.
Does it sound human?
Yes. Our proprietary models are trained on high-fidelity human interactions to capture breath, pauses, intonation, and emotional nuance. It is often indistinguishable from a human agent.
